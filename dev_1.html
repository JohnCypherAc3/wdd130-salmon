Description of the Run

The Terraria Calamity mod true melee sub-class is one of the more difficult sub-classes in the mod to play. The basis of the class is using only weapons that do true melee damage, which are sword-type weapons that do not launch projectiles.

The difficulty of using this class comes from the fact that many enemies and bosses deal contact damage, while the player must be very close to deal damage. Because of this, damage output is not as consistent as other classes, though when hits connect, the damage is often higher.

The run starts with the player having very small and weak weapons. These early-game weapons are difficult and dangerous to use, especially against bosses that move frequently.

As the game progresses, the player gains access to larger weapons and some that launch projectiles upon contact. However, these still require direct hits to be effective.

In the late game, defeating Supreme Calamitas unlocks enchantments such as the “Tainted” enchantment. This allows weapons to function as true melee, although often with reduced range.

Overall, this challenge is difficult but rewarding. While some bosses may feel nearly impossible early on, the experience becomes much more enjoyable later in the game.

Early Game

Very limited weapons with low range and high risk. Survival and patience are key.

Middle Game

Improved weapon variety and better survivability, though bosses remain challenging.

End Game

Access to enchantments and powerful gear makes the playstyle more viable and enjoyable.
